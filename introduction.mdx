## Overview

Market Trak Pro empowers UK estate agents with hyper-local property market insights. You generate professional reports, capture seller leads, and create social media content that positions you as the local expert. Built on live UK property data, the platform automates content creation so you focus on closing deals.

Access your dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com` after signing up. Start your 14-day free trial to explore unlimited report generation and lead capture tools.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes. Follow these steps to generate your first market report.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`. Use your agency email for instant access to UK-wide data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Area" icon="map-pin">
    Choose your postcode or area. Market Trak Pro pulls the latest sales, prices, and trends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Report" icon="file-text">
    Pick a format like Seller's Guide. Customize branding and download or share instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share and Track" icon="eye">
    Post to social media or email clients. Monitor lead views and conversions in your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
