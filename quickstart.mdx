<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
You need a free 14-day trial account to get started. No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

- Access to a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari recommended)
- Your postcode or local area details for hyper-local reports
- Social media accounts for sharing (optional but recommended)
</Callout>

## Sign Up for Your Free Trial

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Go to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Start 14-Day Free Trial**.

    <Image
      src="https://chmqnfglegtdqnhljpwu.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/uploads/headshot-1.jpg"
      alt="Market Trak Pro dashboard login screen"
      width="800"
      height="400"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Enter your email, create a password, and verify your account via the confirmation email.

    You will receive instant access to live UK property data for your area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Patch" icon="map-pin">
    Enter your postcode (e.g., SW1A 1AA) to set up your local market area.

    This powers all your hyper-local reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate Your First Report

Create a professional Seller's Guide or Data Sheet in seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Report Type" icon="file-text">
    From the dashboard, select **Seller's Guide** for lead-focused content or **Data Sheet** for quick stats.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize" icon="settings">
    Add your branding, adjust the postcode radius, and preview.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate & Download" icon="download">
    Click **Generate Report**. Download PDF or get shareable link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Reports use live UK property data, always current and accurate.
</Callout>
