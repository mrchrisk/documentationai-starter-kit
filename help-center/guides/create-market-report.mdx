## Overview

Generate a basic report from your data in Market Trak Pro.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Import data file">
    ````tip
    Go to Data > Import, upload your CSV/XLSX file.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select analysis type">
    Choose "Market Overview" or another template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate report">
    Click "Create Report". View results in the Reports tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>
