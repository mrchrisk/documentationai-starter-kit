## Overview

New to Market Trak Pro? Here’s how to begin:

## Steps

1. Sign up at `https://dashboard.markettrak.com`
2. Activate your account via the email link
3. Log in and access the Dashboard
4. Import your first market data file (CSV, XLSX)
5. Explore the sample reports
